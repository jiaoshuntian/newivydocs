# Monitor & Manage

Once your IvorySQL cluster is running, keeping it healthy and performant requires ongoing attention. This section covers the essential operational tasks: monitoring database activity and performance, performing routine maintenance like vacuuming and log rotation, and managing upgrades, backups, and high-availability configurations.

IvorySQL requires periodic maintenance to stay performant. Vacuuming reclaims dead tuple space, reindexing keeps indexes efficient, and log rotation prevents disk exhaustion. This page covers all routine maintenance tasks.
